Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept for online orders?

We accept payment via credit/debit cards, PayPal, and online payment gateways. Cash on delivery (COD) options may be available in select locations.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times vary depending on your location and order volume. Typically, orders are delivered within 30 minutes.

Do you offer contactless delivery?

Yes, we offer contactless delivery to ensure the safety of our customers and delivery personnel. Simply request contactless delivery when placing your order online.

Can I customize my pizza?

Absolutely! You can customize your pizza by choosing your preferred toppings, crust, and size when placing your order online.

What is your policy on returns or refunds?

We strive for customer satisfaction. If you're not completely satisfied with your order, please contact us within 24 hours, and we'll do our best to resolve any issues or provide a refund or replacement.

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes, we offer gluten-free and vegan pizza options to accommodate dietary preferences. Look for these options when customizing your pizza online.
